10 - Font style

* `font-style` property allows us to apply different styles to classes

* can use `font-style: italic;` to apply italics, or use <i></i> tags
* <em></em> tag (emphasis) does the same as <i> for text, but also applies extra vocal emphasis for screen readers

* similarly, <b> and <strong> do similar things, but screen readers will also says text in a lower tone for <strong>
